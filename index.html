About Chika

Chika Onyebueke was born in Louisiana, but has lived in the South all his life. Form June 2006 to May 2014, Chika served in the USAF as a AC-130 Gunship engine mechanic. After leaving the military, Chika wanted to learn a new skill. Since Chika has always been a copmuter geek, he went to enrolled in Georgia State University as a Computer Science major.
